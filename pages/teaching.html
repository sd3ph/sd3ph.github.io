My Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching is grounded in the belief that the interface between Physics and Artificial Intelligence provides a unique playground for intuitive understanding. I emphasize:

  • Active Learning: Live coding sessions, simulation workshops, and "playground" style problem solving.
  • Interdisciplinary Thinking: Encouraging students to see mathematical structures common to quantum mechanics and machine learning.
  • Project-Based Assessment: Evaluating understanding through tangible models and tools.
